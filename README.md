# Brandon Corbett

I am just trying sling some code and big a good dude!

Over the years I have worked in environments that demand rigor and reliability, including roles at AWS, IBM, and NASA. Those experiences shaped how I think about systems design, security, and operational discipline. But one thing that was always missing were people who actually cared about what they were building and the people who are using it.

I promised myself that my company would be different and I have been focusing on building authentication systems, cyber security tooling, devops and infrastructure tooling that prioritizes the people who use them and the users they serve. 

I am currently working on a brand I have been calling Seamless Auth, an open source passwordless authentication system designed to be embedded directly into applications without redirect flows or hidden dependencies.
